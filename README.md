# Lifelink – Organ Sharing & Management System

## Overview

Lifelink is a healthcare-based academic major project designed to improve transparency and efficiency in organ donation and allocation.

This platform connects:

- Organ Donors
- Organ Recipients
- Hospitals
- Administrators

through a centralized management system.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional organ donation systems often face challenges such as:

- Lack of centralized donor records
- Delays in donor-recipient matching
- Limited transparency in organ availability
- Inefficient coordination between hospitals

---

## Solution

Lifelink provides a digital healthcare platform to:

✔ Manage donor registrations  
✔ Track recipient requests  
✔ Monitor organ availability  
✔ Improve donor-recipient matching  
✔ Support healthcare workflow optimization  

---
